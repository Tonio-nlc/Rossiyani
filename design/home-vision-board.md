# Rossiyani Home Vision Board

## Purpose

The Home page is not a library catalogue.

The Home page is the user's language learning workspace.

Its role is to answer one question:

"What should I do next?"

The homepage is the operational center of Rossiyani.

---

## Identity

Rossiyani has two distinct interface surfaces.

### Workspace Surface

Used for:

- Home
- Practice
- Vocabulary
- Explorer
- Future analytics pages

Inspiration:

- Readwise
- Linear
- Notion
- Duolingo Super
- Stitch Dashboard Vision Board

Characteristics:

- White cards
- Large radius (20–28px)
- Comfortable spacing
- Visible progress
- Strong hierarchy
- Action-oriented
- Clear next steps
- Motivational without gamification

---

### Editorial Surface

Used for:

- Reader
- Manual
- Long-form text pages

Inspiration:

- Kindle
- New Yorker
- Economist
- Premium book reader

Characteristics:

- Editorial rhythm
- Dense content
- Minimal UI
- Reading-first experience

---

## Section Order

1. Continue Learning Hero

Large dominant card.

70/30 split layout.

Left:

Current text cover.

Right:

- title
- collection
- level
- progress
- next milestone
- continue button

---

2. Learning Metrics

Right-side stack:

- streak
- next milestone
- learning progress
- daily activity

These cards are mandatory.

---

3. Featured Collection

One collection only.

Never show multiple collections.

Purpose:

Guide user toward a learning path.

---

4. Today's Practice

Three cards:

- Sentence Builder
- Context Translation
- Vocabulary Review

Action-oriented.

---

5. Recommended Reading

Horizontal reading recommendations.

Maximum 3 cards.

Based on user level.

---

6. Continue Exploration

Secondary tools:

- Explorer
- Saved Words
- Manual

Support section only.

Never dominate the page.
