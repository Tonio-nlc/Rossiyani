# Rossiyani Product Identity

Rossiyani is a modern language learning workspace.

The product combines:

- structured reading
- vocabulary acquisition
- grammar discovery
- guided practice

The goal is not to build a digital library.

The goal is not to build a reading archive.

The goal is to help users make daily progress in Russian.

Every page must answer:

"What is my next learning action?"

Rossiyani should feel:

- modern
- motivating
- approachable
- rewarding
- intelligent

Rossiyani should not feel:

- academic
- institutional
- textbook-like
- archival
- scholarly
