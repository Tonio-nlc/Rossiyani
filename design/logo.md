# ROSSIYANI — LOGO SYSTEM

Version 2.0

This document defines the complete visual identity of the Rossiyani logo.

The objective is not to create a decorative mark.

The objective is to create a timeless editorial symbol that can live independently from the wordmark.

---

# 1. PHILOSOPHY

Rossiyani is not a startup.

Rossiyani is not an AI company.

Rossiyani is not a language app.

Rossiyani is an editorial exploration platform.

The logo must communicate:

understanding

connections

knowledge

exploration

clarity

relationships

depth

precision

---

# 2. DESIGN OBJECTIVE

The logo should feel like the mark of a publishing house or an encyclopedia.

It should be able to exist for decades without redesign.

It should become recognizable through repetition rather than complexity.

---

# 3. WHAT THE LOGO MUST NEVER REPRESENT

Never use:

Russian flag

Kremlin

Saint Basil

Matryoshka

Bear

Double eagle

Hammer & sickle

Cyrillic gimmicks

Speech bubbles

Artificial intelligence symbols

Graduation caps

Books

Globes

Owl

Brain

Chat icon

Robot

---

# 4. DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Simple

Geometric

Editorial

Minimal

Balanced

Timeless

Scalable

Memorable

Calm

Premium

---

# 5. CONCEPT

Rossiyani is a graph of knowledge.

The logo should express:

one element

connecting

to another

creating understanding.

The visual language is:

points

lines

nodes

relationships

paths

---

# 6. SYMBOL DIRECTIONS

Direction A (Preferred)

Minimal graph.

Three nodes connected by two lines.

Not mathematical.

Elegant.

Organic.

Feels handwritten.

Represents:

Word

↓

Concept

↓

Understanding

---

Direction B

Compass.

Very abstract.

Suggests exploration.

No literal compass illustration.

Only directional geometry.

---

Direction C

Editorial annotation mark.

Inspired by margin references.

Very minimal.

Looks like a symbol found inside an encyclopedia.

---

# 7. WORDMARK

The wordmark should be independent.

Simple.

Elegant.

Editorial.

No effects.

No outline.

No gradients.

No distortions.

Perfect letter spacing.

---

# 8. TYPOGRAPHIC STYLE

Editorial serif.

High contrast.

Quiet.

Sophisticated.

Feels printed rather than digital.

The wordmark should look equally beautiful on:

paper

screen

book cover

favicon page

documentation

---

# 9. SYMBOL

The symbol must work without text.

Someone should eventually recognize Rossiyani from the symbol alone.

Properties:

balanced

centered

minimal

symmetrical or optically symmetrical

clear at 16px

recognizable at 512px

---

# 10. FAVICON

The favicon is NOT the full logo.

It is only the symbol.

Requirements:

works at 16x16

works in monochrome

works on paper background

works on dark background

recognizable immediately

---

# 11. LOGO LOCKUPS

[Symbol] Rossiyani

---

Rossiyani

---

Standalone

Symbol only.

Used for:

favicon

avatar

watermark

app icon

---

# 12. COLOR USAGE

Preferred:

Charcoal

#1F1F1D

---

Alternative:

Slate Blue

#5F768D

Only for editorial contexts.

---

Monochrome

100% black

100% white

must always work.

---

No gradients.

No shadows.

No glow.

No textures.

---

# 13. CLEAR SPACE

Minimum clear space:

equal to the height of one node of the symbol.

Nothing should visually compete with the logo.

---

# 14. MINIMUM SIZE

Wordmark

120px

---

Symbol

16px minimum

24px preferred

32px ideal

---

# 15. DO NOT

Do not rotate.

Do not stretch.

Do not add shadows.

Do not add outlines.

Do not place inside circles unnecessarily.

Do not add background effects.

Do not animate.

Do not recolor randomly.

---

# 16. MOTION

If animated:

fade

draw

connection

line expansion

maximum duration:

400ms

Never bounce.

Never spin.

Never scale aggressively.

---

# 17. BRAND APPLICATIONS

Navigation

Documentation

Website

Reader

Manual

Explorer

Import

Print

PDF

Social

Presentation

The logo should feel equally natural everywhere.

---

# 18. VISUAL ASSOCIATIONS

The logo should evoke:

an editorial reference mark

a linguistic graph

a quiet publishing house

a research notebook

a carefully annotated encyclopedia

---

# 19. SUCCESS TEST

Hide the interface.

Keep only:

paper background

logo

typography

one separator

If the product still feels like Rossiyani,

the identity is strong.

---

# 20. NORTH STAR

The Rossiyani logo should not become famous because it is original.

It should become famous because, after hundreds of hours of reading,

users naturally associate it with:

clarity,

understanding,

deep learning,

and the pleasure of exploring a language.
