Every screen follows these principles.

1

Content first

The content is always visually dominant.

2

Progressive disclosure

Secondary information stays hidden until useful.

3

Monotasking

Only one primary action per screen.

4

Editorial rhythm

Whitespace is part of the interface.

5

Consistency

Every page belongs to the same publication.

6

Russian first

Russian typography always receives visual emphasis.

7

Calm interaction

No visual noise.

No unnecessary badges.

No decorative UI.

No distracting gradients.
